About the App

This app was designed to help people get to and from work a little bit more efficiently. The app provides expected commute data, obtained free of charge from Google, in graphs and gives some simple analysis of the best times to compute in a textual form. To estimate the commute time, the app queries the API for travel time estimates on an average Wednesday, disregarding daily traffic and other chance events. The Commuter App was built in Python and employs a variety of 3rd-party APIs, CDNs, and general utilities. These external utilities are gratefully acknowledged in the table below.
